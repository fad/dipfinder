Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot My Password?" on the login form and follow the instructions sent to your email.

How many stocks can I track in DipFinder watchlist?

Guest users can track up to 5 stocks. Logged-in users can track up to 8 stocks.

Is there a subscription plan?

Not yet. We are still working on more features and improvements. In the meantime, you can enjoy DipFinder free of ads and at no cost.

How often is stock data updated?

We try to update the data every 5 minutes. DipFinder does not provide real-time data and we do not guarantee data accuracy.

Can I make a suggestion or report an issue?

Yes, please contact us.

Do you offer investment advice?

No, DipFinder is a tool for tracking stocks. We do not provide investment advice.
