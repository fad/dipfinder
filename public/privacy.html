Use of Vercel is based on our legitimate interest in reliable website delivery (Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. f GDPR).

Data We Collect

Account data

If you register, we store your email address and a hashed (bcrypt) password in our database (MongoDB). No payment information is collected or stored. Legal basis: contract performance (Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. b GDPR).

Watchlist data

Your watchlist of stock tickers is stored in your browser's localStorage. If you are logged in, it is also stored on our servers to sync across devices. Watchlists contain only ticker symbols - no personal data.

Authentication tokens

After login, a JWT (JSON Web Token) is stored in your browser's localStorage. The token contains your email address and an expiry date. We do not use cookies for authentication.

Technical data

Standard server logs may include your IP address, browser type, and page access times. This data is used solely to operate the service and is not used for profiling.

Email

If you contact us by email, we retain your address to enable continued communication.

Third-Party Services

Yahoo Finance

Stock prices and moving averages are fetched from Yahoo Finance. Only ticker symbols are transmitted - no personal data. See Yahoo's Privacy Policy.

Analytics (Umami)

We use Umami Analytics, a cookie-free, privacy-friendly analytics tool. It collects only anonymised usage statistics (pages visited, browser type). No personal data is stored or shared with third parties.

CAPTCHA (Cloudflare Turnstile)

The registration form uses Cloudflare Turnstile to prevent automated abuse. Your IP address may be transmitted to Cloudflare, Inc. (101 Townsend St, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA). See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
