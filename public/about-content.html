About Dip Finder

Find the dip. Skip the noise.

Most investing tools feel like they were built for people who enjoy staring at 47 indicators at once.

Dip Finder is different.

We help you answer one simple question:

Are any of the stocks I already like currently trading at a discount?

That's it.

How it works

Dip Finder tracks the stocks on your watchlist and compares their current prices to their 200-day moving average.

Think of the 200-day moving average as a stock's long-term trend line. When a stock drops below that line, it may be in a dip.

Not always a screaming buy. Not financial advice. Not magic. But definitely something worth looking at.

Your watchlist, ranked by dip

Instead of checking every stock one by one, Dip Finder puts your watchlist into a simple chart. You can quickly see which stocks are close to their trend, which are above it, and which are sitting deep in dip territory.

Want to dig deeper?

Once something catches your eye, use the Stock Screener to explore more details and compare other stocks. Dip Finder helps you find the signal. You do the research.

What's the catch?

No catch. The basic version is free, and you can start right away without creating an account.

We plan to add optional Pro features like personalized dip alerts and watchlist-based email updates — so instead of constantly checking the market, the dips can come to you.
