About Dip Finder

Smart investing shouldn't be complicated.

At Dip Finder, we believe smart investing shouldn't be complicated. That's why we've created a straightforward yet powerful tool designed to help you effortlessly spot opportunities in the market.

How does it work?

We track your favorite stocks and compare their current prices to their 200-day moving averages.

When a stock's price falls below this average, it signals a potential buying opportunity — also known as a "dip."

Your watchlist stocks are clearly displayed on an intuitive chart, letting you quickly identify the most promising dips.

Want to dive deeper? Our Stock Screener helps you research these opportunities further.

It's investing made easy: no fuss, no jargon — just the dips you're looking for.

What's the catch?

There's no catch! Our basic service is completely free, and you can start right away, even without creating an account.

In the future, we plan to offer additional Pro features, including personalized dip alerts delivered directly to your inbox, for a small subscription fee.
