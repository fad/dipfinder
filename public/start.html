How it works

Step 1

Build your watchlist

Add the tickers you've been watching - stocks you'd buy if the price was right. No account needed to get started.

Step 2

Pick a moving average

Choose from the 10, 50, 100, or 200-day SMA. Each tells a different story - short-term pullback or long-term undervaluation.

Step 3

Spot the dips

Your watchlist is ranked by how far each stock is below its average. The deepest dips rise to the top - no math required.
